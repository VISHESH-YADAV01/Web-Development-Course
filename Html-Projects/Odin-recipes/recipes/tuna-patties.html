Tuna Patties


Description:

Tuna patties are delicious and versatile seafood delicacies made from canned tuna. These savory delights are typically prepared by combining flaked tuna with ingredients such as breadcrumbs, eggs, onions, herbs, and spices. The mixture is formed into patties and pan-fried until golden and crispy on the outside. Tuna patties offer a delightful balance of flavors, with the tuna providing a rich and savory taste. They can be enjoyed as a standalone dish, served with a tangy dipping sauce, or as a filling for sandwiches or burgers. These protein-packed treats make for a satisfying and healthy meal option for seafood enthusiasts.

Ingredients

  • Canned tuna
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Eggs
  • Onion
  • Herbs (such as parsley or dill)
  • Spices (such as garlic powder or paprika)
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)

Step-by-Step Recipe:

  1. Drain the canned tuna and transfer it to a mixing bowl.
  2. Add 1/2 cup breadcrumbs to the bowl.
  3. Add 1/4 cup finely chopped onion to the bowl.
  4. Crack 2 eggs into the bowl.
  5. Add 2 tablespoons of chopped fresh parsley, 1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder, 1/2 teaspoon of paprika, and salt and pepper to taste to the bowl.
  6. Mix all the ingredients together until well combined. You can use a fork or your hands for this step.
  7. Shape the mixture into patties. This recipe typically makes around 4 medium-sized patties.
  8. Heat a skillet or frying pan over medium heat and add a drizzle of oil.
  9. Place the patties onto the skillet and cook for about 3-4 minutes on each side or until golden brown and crispy.
  10. Once cooked, remove the tuna patties from the skillet and place them on a paper towel to absorb any excess oil.
  11. Serve the tuna patties as desired. They can be enjoyed on their own, in a sandwich, or with a side of salad or dipping sauce.
Note: Feel free to adjust the seasonings and quantities according to your taste preferences. Enjoy your homemade tuna patties!
